Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and the expense records you create (amounts, categories, dates, and notes). We do not collect payment information or link to your bank accounts.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service to you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is accessible only to you through your account. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This request is made directly from your browser and is subject to Google's privacy policy. We do not integrate with any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
